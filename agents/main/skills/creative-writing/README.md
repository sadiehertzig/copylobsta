# Creative Writing

Collaborative creative writing assistant for stories, worldbuilding, character development, and feedback on your work.

## Usage

- "Help me write a short story about a time traveler"
- "I'm building a fantasy world — help me figure out the magic system"
- "Can you help me develop this character?"
- "Read this and tell me what you think" (paste your writing)

## Modes

- **Free Write** — write stories, poems, scenes together
- **Worldbuilding** — build fictional worlds, settings, magic systems
- **Character Development** — create and deepen characters
- **Feedback** — get constructive critique on your writing

## Philosophy

Your voice is sacred. The bot writes WITH you, not FOR you. It mirrors your style, suggests directions when you're stuck, and gives honest but encouraging feedback.

## Dependencies

None. Pure prompt-instruction skill.
