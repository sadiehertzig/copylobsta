# Notes Quiz

Turn photos of your handwritten or typed notes into interactive quizzes. Upload a picture of your notes and get quizzed on the content.

## Usage

Upload a photo of your notes and say:
- "Quiz me on these notes"
- "Test me on this"
- "/notesquiz"

## How It Works

1. Upload one or more photos of your notes
2. The bot reads and extracts key concepts from the images
3. Generates 5-10 mixed question types (multiple choice, fill-in-the-blank, short answer, true/false)
4. Quizzes you one question at a time with running score
5. Gives a summary at the end highlighting what to review

## Dependencies

Requires an image-capable model (the bot needs to be able to read images).
